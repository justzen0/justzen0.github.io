Food must not be looked as a source of pleasure. It is dead plants and animals on your plate when you have to consume in order to survive. food is necessary for survival, but being gluttonous is not good. 
जीने के लिए खाओ, खाने के लिए नहीं जीयो। (Eat to live, don't live to eat)
